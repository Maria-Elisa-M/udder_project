Point clouds:

-raw (array): xyz coordinated of the udder as in the image indicated by the mask
-udder (array): xyz coordinates of the udder after tilitng and removing outliers
-quarters (dictionary): xyz coordinates of each quater, region indicated by the watershed labels, after tilting and removing outliers
-keypoints (dictionary):  xyz coordinates of each teat after tilitng
-teat (dictionary): xyx of observed, predicted and teat points used for teat lenght estimation
